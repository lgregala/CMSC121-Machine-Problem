ABOUT US

Garden of Melon is a cozy haven for plant lovers, offering a curated selection of lush greenery, stylish pots, and plant care essentials to bring life and calm into every home. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting your indoor garden journey, we are here to help you grow with confidence. From tiny succulents to towering indoor trees, we believe every plant has a story — and we are here to help you tell yours.

THE BIRTH

It all began in 2019, in a tiny apartment with a single potted monstera and too much sunlight. Melon, a college student stressed from school, started collecting plants as a way to unwind. What was meant to be just one or two quickly turned into a full-blown indoor jungle. With every leaf that unfurled, the idea grew: what if this love for greenery could be shared with more people? By 2021, Melon turned her hobby into a small online shop: selling propagated plants, pot kits, and handwritten care notes. It is a story of growth, rooted in self-care, community, and the belief that even the smallest seed can grow into something beautiful.

MISSION & VISION

To inspire and nurture a love for indoor gardening by providing thoughtfully selected plants, stylish essentials, and heartfelt guidance. We believe that caring for plants is a calming and rewarding experience that everyone can enjoy.

To grow a greener world, one cozy space at a time where every home becomes a sanctuary filled with life. We envision a community that embraces plants not just as décor, but as living companions that bring peace & purpose. With every plant we share, we hope to cultivate connection that extends far beyond the pot.
