ABOUT US

Garden of Melon is a cozy haven for plant lovers, offering a curated selection of lush greenery, stylish pots, and plant care essentials to bring life and calm into every home. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting your indoor garden journey, we are here to help you grow with confidence. From tiny succulents to towering indoor trees, we believe every plant has a story — and we are here to help you tell yours.

OUR STORY

It all began in 2019, in a tiny apartment with a single potted monstera and too much sunlight. Melon, a college student stressed from school, started collecting plants as a way to unwind. What was meant to be just one or two quickly turned into a full-blown indoor jungle. With every leaf that unfurled, the idea grew: what if this love for greenery could be shared with more people? By 2021, Melon turned her hobby into a small online shop: selling propagated plants, pot kits, and handwritten care notes. It is a story of growth, rooted in self-care, community, and the belief that even the smallest seed can grow into something beautiful. As more plant lovers joined the journey, Garden of Melon began to blossom into something bigger.

In 2023, Melon opened her first physical store: a sun-drenched nook filled with gentle music, leafy shadows, and the same comforting vibe that started it all. The shop became more than a store — it became a sanctuary. A space for learning, healing, and remembering that growth takes time. Now, Garden of Melon continues to thrive with a passionate team, a growing family of customers, and a mission to bring the joy of plants into every home. What started as a quiet escape became a blooming movement, proving that the most beautiful things can sprout from the most unexpected places. Every pot, sprout, and leaf tells a story, and Garden of Melon is here to help grow yours.

AWARD-WINNING PLANTS

1. Calathea

A group of tropical plants known for their stunning, patterned leaves that often have a purplish underside. Nicknamed the Prayer Plant because many species close their leaves at night, like they're praying. They're a bit fussy but totally worth it for looks.

2. Peace Lily

Also known as Spathiphyllum, a graceful houseplant known for its dark green leaves and elegant white, sail-like flowers. It’s not a true lily but is prized for its beauty and air-purifying abilities. Peace Lilies also symbolize peace and healing.

3. African Violet

Also known as Saintpaulia, a compact flowering plant with fuzzy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, colorful blooms (purple, pink, white, etc.). They’re popular for their long blooming period and are easy to grow on windowsills.

4. Monstera Deliciosa

Also known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, it's a tropical vine native to Central America, famous for its large, glossy green leaves with natural holes or slits. It's fast-growing and gives off major jungle vibes, making it a favorite statement plant.

5. Bird of Paradise

It is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa, known for its large, banana-like leaves and extraordinary flowers that resemble the shape of a colorful bird in flight. The vibrant orange and blue blooms make it a show-stopper.
